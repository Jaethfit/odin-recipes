Natashas unstealable meatloaf recipe

Ingredients:
  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 3tbsb fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1.3 cup milk
  • 1.5 tsp salt, italian seasoning
  • 1.2 tsp pepper and paprika

instructions

  1. Line a loaf pan with oil
  2. Add all the ingredients and beat the devil out of it
  3. bake it for 40 minutes at 375 degrees F
  4. dunk on your friends about how good it is
